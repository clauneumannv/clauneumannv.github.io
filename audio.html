For our second project, we needed to create a 2-3 minute sound piece that "takes us on a journey". Rob, Amber, and I developed a piece structured as a story that lives on a web page and includes original work. Whether you are a current or former NYUAD student or you would are planning to become part of the community in the future, Forty Acres will take you to an immersive experience to explore your senses and the magic of NYUAD. The suitcase' wheels rolling against the concrete make us imagine the ongoing journey of an NYUAD student.
