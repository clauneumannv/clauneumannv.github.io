For our last group project, we were asked to create a 2 minute Animation/Effects video with sound that lives on a web page and incorporates some form of interactivity. Manas, Mateo, and I decided to capture what happens in the library after it closes through a stop motion video. We created the video using the program "Dragon Frame" and edited it with Adobe Premiere. A red book is forgotten on one of the tables, and now it needs to find its family with the help of some friends.
